Pasta
Delicious combination of pasta

Cheese pasta can be customized by adding ingredients like cooked vegetables (spinach, mushrooms, broccoli), cooked meats (chicken, bacon), or spices (red pepper flakes, paprika) to suit your taste preferences. It's a versatile and satisfying dish that is perfect for a quick and comforting meal

Manchurian
Vegetable Manchurian Balls:

Garlic: Finely chopped or minced garlic cloves. Ginger: Finely chopped or grated ginger. Green chilies: Chopped green chilies for spice (adjust according to your preference). Soy sauce: Adds a rich and salty flavor to the sauce. Tomato ketchup: Provides sweetness and tanginess to the sauce. Cornflour slurry: Cornflour mixed with water to thicken the sauce. Spring onions: Chopped spring onions for garnish. Salt and pepper: For seasoning the sauce.

Momos
Vegetable Momos

Tomato chutney or hot sauce: Typically served as a dipping sauce with momos. Schezwan sauce (optional): Adds spicy flavor to the momos. Spring onions: Chopped spring onions can be added to the filling for extra flavor. Ajinomoto (MSG): Some recipes include Ajinomoto for added umami flavor, but it's optional. Vinegar: A splash of vinegar can be added to the filling for tanginess.
